Roman Senninger

I am an Associate Professor of Political Science at Aarhus University. My research focuses on political elites and political institutions in domestic and European Union politics.

Methodologically, I have a strong interest in (quasi-) experiments, computational text analysis, and spatial and geographic data analysis.

I hold a Semper Ardens Accelerate Grant from the Carlsberg Foundation for the project ‘How and Why Politicians Learn From Policies from Abroad’.
