Roman Senninger

I am an Associate Professor of Political Science at Aarhus University.

My research centers on political elites, institutions, and public opinion in Europe. Methodologically, I have a strong interest in experiments and computational social science.

I am currently holding a three-year Semper Ardens Accelerate Grant from the Carlsberg Foundation.
