Roman Senninger



I am an Associate Professor of Political Science at Aarhus University.

My research focuses on political elites and institutions in Europe. Methodologically, I have a strong interest in (quasi-) experiments and computational social science.

Currently, I’m holding a three-year Semper Ardens Accelerate Grant from the Carlsberg Foundation.
